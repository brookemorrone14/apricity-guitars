Apricity Guitar Company - Official Cite

Official guitar building company, founded and managed by Andy Morrone. All guitars designed and built by Andy Morrone.

About Apricity Guitar Co, and it's Founder:

Apricity Guitars was founded by Andy Morrone, a father of two, based in Perth, Ontario. A former Canadian Armed Forces soldier, with a passion for music, specifically the acoustic guitar. In his late 40's, the idea to build guitars was planted, and Apricity Guitars has only further developed as Andy develops his skills.

Andy is a kind-hearted individual, who continuously strives to help others. He has been a part of two non-profit organizations: Spectre Gunship and Concerts for Kids, both projects to rasie money for the Ontario children's hospitals; Cheo and SickKids. Despite pursuing his career elsewhere, Andy has always had a passion for music, and hopes to eventually create child-size guitars for the children's hospitals stated above.

Andy is still in the early stages of his business, and currently in the process of learning how to create custom guitars of his own. Thus far, Andy has selected the wood for his first guitar, shaped and moulded the wood, and added the necessary elements to create the desired sound.
